Bergen Ship Agency acts as owners agent for **tank and supply** vessel operations. Independence from other shipping companies allows us to act in our principal's best interest, with the confidentiality and discretion the role requires.

Our experience handling tankers, supply ships, and rigs — combined with established connections to Norwegian authorities and access to all terminals and refineries on the western part of Norway — supports the operation from port call planning through disbursement.
